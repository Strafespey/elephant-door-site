# 6 - Goblins strike back.

This is an area that's just going to take some polish.  I added goblin animations to the game (just reusing some of the free placeholder ones I already have).  Them walking around hunched over looks cool, but the attacks look pretty terrible.  First of all, all of the attacks I have them use seem to have some sort of angle baked into them that makes the targetting off when the player is standing still.  Second, I have to tune the distance they choose to attack at very precisely (otherwise they miss even harder).  I'll probably try to get some snappier animations for the enemy NPCs - or if I do use wide swings like these, I'll tune the angle and distance precisely for each attack and probably let them cheat with bigger hitboxes.  I'm trying to find a nice balance between an action game and an ARPG.
